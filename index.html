New Players

Welcome to the game of X-Wing!

Here, you will be able to find everything you need to get acquainted with the game of X-Wing and start flying!

We recommend you check out the following videos to get a feel for the basics of the game.

On this site we also have some helpful links for creating custom squads and printing them out.

Make sure to join our discord to ask any questions and on where to get either starter kits, ships, or anything else you may need to get set up.

The best and most fun way to learn is by playing, so make sure to check out our schedule to see what times and at what locations we play. Don't be afraid to join us, as we all love teaching new players!
